About Alpha Gamma Delta

What is Alpha Gamma Delta?

Alpha Gamma Delta was founded at Syracuse University on May 30th, 1904 by 11 empowering women. The Zeta Eta chapter was founded at Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute on May 10th, 1980, celebrating 44 years of our motto, "Loving, Leading, and Lasting." Alpha Gamma Delta is one of three social sororites on RPI's campus. Our colors are red, buff, and green, and our mascot is the squirrel.
