    Alumni Newsletter

    The Zeta Eta Chapter every year releases an alumni newsletter created and sent out by our Administrations Team. The newsletter contains a summary of our year, with details such as recruitment, philanthropy, and our graduating seniors for that year. If you are an Alumni interested in updates and newsletters, please add yourself to the Facebook group where the Administration team sends out all current information about the Zeta Eta chapter!

    Alumni Donations

    We graciously accept any monetary and physical donation items such as wooden letters, t-shirts, craft supplies, and anything that the Zeta Eta Chapter could give a home and more love to. Monetary-wise, we have a link to a Zeffy, which will directly go to the Zeta Eta Chapter (please make sure to specify what the donation money is for).
    Zeffy

Any donations will recieve a mention in the Alumni newsletter of that year. If you have any questions about donations, don't hesitate to contact our Administration Team. Thank you to all of our Alumni that have contributed to making the Zeta Eta chapter better each day.
