Involvement

Every Alpha Gam contributes to the world's work in the community where they are placed. Whether it's on campus or around the world, Alpha Gam's are involved and thrive anywhere they can. Outside of Alpha Gamma Delta, members are involved in various clubs and organizations on campus, such as:
  • Community Service: RPI Ambulance, Habitat for Humanity
  • Sports & Recreation: Track & Field, Tennis, Cheerleading, Crew, Softball, Dance Team, Ballroom Dance, Intramural Sports
  • Performing Arts: Pep Band, UPAC Concerts
  • Cultural: ASA, ISA, NSBE, SASE, SHPE
  • Professional: Society of Women Engineers (SWE), Women in Buisness (WiB), ACM-W, Women's Mentoring, ALAC Tutoring
  • Media: The Polytechnic, Rensselaer Center for Open Source (RCOS)
  • Honors Society: Phalanx, Order of Omega, Upsilon Pi Epsilon, Alpha Psi Omega
