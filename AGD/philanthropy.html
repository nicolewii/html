Our Philanthropy

Alpha Gamma Delta's philanthropic focus is fighting hunger. 34 million people, including 9 million children in the United States are food insecure. The Zeta Eta Chapter strives to support local communities through our passionate members empowered to fight hunger. We host events such as Alpha Grabba Donut, Phat Sammies, Pretty Pretzels, and Wingin' It, which all help support the Alpha Gamma Delta Foundation, Meals on Wheels, and Feeding America. Like many other Alpha Gamma Delta chapters, we also continue our philanthropic efforts during Hunger Action Month and Week of Service.
The Zeta Eta Chapter has achieved Jewel Society, which honors chapters who have raised $2,000 or more annually for the Alpha Gamma Delta Foundation, since 2018.

Alpha Grabba Donut

Sisters come together to the Alpha Gamma Delta house to frost and decorate donuts that are sold to students all over campus to grab a snack before, after, or on their way to classes!
×

Phat Sammies

A joint philanthropy event with Lambda Chi Alpha (LXA) to create giant (or phat) sandwiches with mozzerella sticks, chicken fingers, mac and cheese, and fries all on a roll. They are handmade, sold and delivered to various students on campus for a late night meal!
×

Pretty Pretzels

Around Valentine's Day, sisters dip and decorate pretzels in various chocolates and sprinkles for students on campus to send a Valentine or have a sweet treat for themselves!
×

Wingin' It

A joint philanthropy event with Pi Kappa Theta (Ski Lodge) which involves selling and delivering wings of various flavors and fries until 2:00 AM for students on campus!
×

Dog Days

A joint wellness philanthropy event with Chi Phi, encouraging students to meet both of the organizations, pet therapy dogs, and get a breath of fresh air during the week of finals in the Spring!
